Frequently Asked Question
How to order ?
To make an order, just select the desired item then click the "Buy Now" button to proceed to the payment directly, or select the "Cart" button to add items to the cart if you want to continue viewing other stuff. If you already want to make a payment for the items in the cart, select the basket icon and proceed with the payment transaction.
Can i return an item ?
Returns can only be made if the item received has a physical defect.
How can i know my order has been shipped ?
The delivery status will change to "Delivered" if the item has been shipped.
How much is the shipping cost ?
The shipping cost depends on the destination address, so it varies for each destination.
How long will it take to receive my order ?
Estimated shipping time depends on the distance of delivery or destination city with a period of 3-7 working days.
Can i exchange an item ?
Exchanging an item can only be done if the item received have physical defect.
When is my order being processed ?
Your order will be processed on the same day if the payment is made before 12 noon. If the payment is made after 12 noon, the order will be processed on the next day.
Can i cancel my order ?
If the payment has not been made, you can cancel your order. If the payment has been made, your order can not be cancelled.
